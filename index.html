About the Conference

The 1st International Conference focuses on the transformative potential of Artificial Intelligence and Emerging Technologies to address global challenges and craft a progressive future. Join us for an interdisciplinary exploration into the impact of AI on humanity.

Tracks and Themes

AI - Human Behavior & Social Impact

Explore AI's role in social influence, decision-making, emotions, and networks.

AI for Language, Culture, and Public Policy

Dive into NLP, multilingualism, and AI's role in governance and cultural preservation.

Digital Humanities and AI in Management

Discover AI's potential in historical research, art, and management support.

The conference will feature keynote sessions, technical presentations, panel discussions, and workshops. A detailed schedule will be shared soon.
